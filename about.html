It's so nice to meet you!

I'm a pelvic floor occupational therapist specializing in complex chronic pelvic pain. I believe it is a privilege to do my work.

My therapeutic approach incorporates yoga, functional movement, craniosacral techniques, myofascial release, somatic coaching, and pain reprocessing therapy. Everything I do supports self-efficacy, body-positive care, and trauma-informed treatment — for all genders.

I live in Baltimore with my spouse and twin children. I'm originally from the Hudson Valley, NY, and outside of work you'll find me fermenting things and making art.

I love supporting clients with tough, challenging cases

You're in the right place if…

  • You've had symptoms for a long time and want a partner who'll stay curious with you.
  • You've already seen multiple providers and haven't found the right fit.
  • Your situation feels unexplained, complex, or "tough to treat."
  • You want care that integrates your whole nervous system and biomechanics — not just one body part.
  • You'd rather do deep, lasting self-exploration than chase a quick fix.
