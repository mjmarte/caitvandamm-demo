It's a delight to meet you

A pelvic floor therapist for "tough to treat" bodies.

I'm a pelvic floor therapist (OT) specializing in chronic pelvic pain and "tough to treat" pelvic health care. I treat across the gender spectrum with trauma-informed care, rooted in the belief that bodies are different — full stop.

My approach incorporates nervous-system regulation, biomechanics of the whole body, neuromuscular re-education, and functional movement strategies.
